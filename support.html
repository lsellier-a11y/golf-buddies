Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a golf buddy?

On the Players tab, tap the + button and fill in their details.

How do I log a round?

Open a player, then tap Add Round to record the date, course, and notes.

Why isn't a club showing on the map?

The map locates clubs by name using Apple Maps. If a club can't be found, it will be flagged in the Clubs list. Editing the club to include its city and state usually helps it appear.

How do I back up or move my data?

In Settings, use Export All Data to save a file, and Import from JSON to restore it — including on a new device.

Where is my data stored?

All of your data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
